The repository contains Matlab scripts used to compute analytical solutions 
for the evolution of an incompressible fluid drop under a central force field.


These are divided in FOLDERS as below:


INVISCID 2D Stretching Drop
Compute the EXACT analitical solution for a circular inviscid drop deforming 
under the action of a central force field in TWO spatial DIMENSIONS 


INVISCID 3D Stretching Drop // axial symmetry 
Compute the EXACT analitical solution for a circular inviscid drop deforming 
under the action of a central force field in THREE spatial DIMENSIONS 


LINEARIZED 2D viscous Stretching Drop
Compute the linearized analytical solution for a circular VISCOUS drop deforming 
under the action of a central force field and/or under the action of surface tension in TWO spatial DIMENSIONS. 
It also contains the approximate analytical solution obtained by using the Lamb's method (1932)


LINEARIZED 3D viscous Stretching Drop  // axial symmetry
Compute the approximate analytical solution obtained by using the Lamb's method (1932) for a circular VISCOUS drop deforming 
under the action of a central force field and/or under the action of surface tension in THREE spatial DIMENSIONS. 


Rotating Visous Drop  // single-mode solution
Compute the EXACT analitical solution for a rotating viscous circular cylinder subjected to a radial force
field in TWO spatial dimensions. 



More details are contained in the files themselves.
